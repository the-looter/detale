Affiliate Link Terms & Transparency

At DETALED, we strive to provide genuine, well-researched, and transparent product recommendations. To support our work, we use **affiliate links** to earn a small commission when you make a purchase through our links—at no additional cost to you.

How Affiliate Links Work?

  • When you click on an affiliate link on our site, you are redirected to a trusted e-commerce platform (e.g., Amazon, Best Buy, etc.).
  • If you decide to make a purchase, we receive a small commission as part of an affiliate program.
  • These commissions help us maintain our website, conduct in-depth reviews, and bring you the best recommendations.
  • Affiliate links do **not** impact the price you pay for the product.

Why Affiliate Links Make Our Reviews Genuine & Authentic?

  • Unbiased Recommendations: Our reviews are based on **detailed research**, real user feedback, and technical specifications—not sponsorships.
  • No Additional Cost to You: Clicking our affiliate links doesn’t increase the product price.
  • Encourages Honest Reviews: We only recommend products that meet quality and performance standards.
  • Supports Our Work: Commissions allow us to improve content, test more products, and deliver accurate reviews.
