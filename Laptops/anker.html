Anker 735 Charger - Compact & Powerful Charging Solution

The Anker 735 Charger is a compact yet powerful 65W GaN charger designed to fast-charge multiple devices simultaneously. Ideal for professionals, travelers, and tech enthusiasts who need efficient charging on the go.

  • Compatibility: Laptops, Smartphones, Tablets, and more
  • Fast Charging: Supports PD (Power Delivery) & Quick Charge
  • Weight: 132g
  • Foldable Plug: Yes, for portability
