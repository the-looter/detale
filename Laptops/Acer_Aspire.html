Acer Aspire 5 (2023) - Budget Laptop Review

The **Acer Aspire 5 (2023)** is a powerful yet affordable laptop designed for students, professionals, and casual users. It offers a great balance between performance, design, and battery life, making it one of the best budget-friendly laptops.

Key Specifications

  • Processor: Intel Core i5-1335U / AMD Ryzen 5 7530U
  • Graphics: Intel Iris Xe / AMD Radeon Graphics
  • Display: 15.6" FHD (1920x1080) IPS, 16:9 Aspect Ratio
  • RAM: 8GB / 16GB DDR4 (Upgradeable)
  • Storage: 256GB / 512GB / 1TB SSD
  • Battery: Up to 10 Hours
  • Weight: 3.9 lbs (1.8 kg)
  • Ports: 1x USB-C, 3x USB-A, HDMI 2.0, Ethernet, 3.5mm Audio Jack
  • OS: Windows 11 Home

Unique Features

  • Affordable Pricing: Great performance at a budget-friendly cost.
  • Upgradeable RAM & Storage: Supports up to 32GB RAM & extra SSD slot.
  • Acer BlueLightShield™: Reduces eye strain for longer work sessions.
  • Fast Charging: Charges up to 50% in just 30 minutes.
  • Backlit Keyboard: Comfortable typing experience in low light.

Pros & Cons

Pros:

  • Great value for money.
  • Good battery life for daily usage.
  • Lightweight and portable.
  • Decent performance for office work, browsing, and media consumption.

Cons:

  • Plastic build feels less premium.
  • No dedicated graphics for gaming.
  • Display could be brighter for outdoor use.
