Dell Latitude E5470 - Business Laptop Review

The **Dell Latitude E5470** is a reliable business laptop that delivers solid performance with Intel processors, a durable design, and essential security features. It remains a great option for professionals looking for a budget-friendly work laptop.

Key Specifications

  • Processor: Intel Core i5-6300U / i7-6600U
  • Graphics: Intel HD Graphics 520 / AMD Radeon R7 M360 (Optional)
  • Display: 14" HD (1366x768) / FHD (1920x1080) Anti-glare
  • RAM: 8GB / 16GB DDR4 (Upgradeable)
  • Storage: 256GB / 512GB SSD or 1TB HDD
  • Battery: 62Whr, Up to 8 Hours
  • Weight: 3.88 lbs (1.76 kg)
  • Ports: 3x USB 3.0, HDMI, VGA, Ethernet, SD Card Reader, Smart Card Slot
  • OS: Windows 10 Pro (Upgradeable to Windows 11 with limitations)

Unique Features

  • Military-Grade Durability: Built to withstand harsh business environments.
  • Security Features: TPM 2.0, Fingerprint Scanner, and Smart Card Reader.
  • Upgradeable Components: RAM and storage can be easily upgraded.
  • Great Keyboard: Tactile keyboard with excellent key travel.
  • Business-Friendly Ports: VGA and Ethernet support for legacy connections.

Pros & Cons

Pros:

  • Solid build quality with a professional look.
  • Good performance for office and productivity tasks.
  • Upgradable RAM and storage for longevity.
  • Reliable security features for business users.

Cons:

  • Older design compared to modern ultrabooks.
  • Display quality is average (unless upgraded to FHD).
  • No USB-C port for future-proofing.
