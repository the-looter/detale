ASUS VivoBook 15 (2023) - Budget-Friendly Laptop Review

The ASUS VivoBook 15 (2023) is a stylish and lightweight laptop that offers a balance of performance and affordability, making it perfect for students, professionals, and casual users.

Key Specifications

  • Processor: Intel Core i5-1235U / AMD Ryzen 5 7530U
  • Graphics: Intel Iris Xe / AMD Radeon Graphics
  • Display: 15.6" FHD (1920x1080) IPS, 250 nits Brightness
  • RAM: 8GB / 16GB DDR4 (Upgradeable)
  • Storage: 256GB / 512GB / 1TB SSD
  • Battery: Up to 8 Hours
  • Weight: 3.7 lbs (1.68 kg)
  • Ports: 1x USB-C, 2x USB-A, HDMI, SD Card Reader, 3.5mm Audio Jack
  • OS: Windows 11 Home

Unique Features

  • NanoEdge Display: Ultra-thin bezels for an immersive viewing experience.
  • ErgoLift Hinge: Improves typing comfort and cooling.
  • ASUS SonicMaster Audio: Enhanced sound clarity for music and video.
  • Fingerprint Reader: Secure login with Windows Hello.
  • Fast Charging: 60% battery in just 49 minutes.

Pros & Cons

Pros:

  • Affordable with decent performance.
  • Lightweight and stylish design.
  • Upgradable RAM and storage.
  • Good battery life with fast charging.

Cons:

  • Plastic build may not feel premium.
  • No dedicated GPU for gaming.
  • Display brightness could be better.
