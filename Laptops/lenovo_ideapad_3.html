Lenovo IdeaPad 3 (2023) - Budget-Friendly Laptop Review

The **Lenovo IdeaPad 3 (2023)** is an affordable yet powerful laptop that is ideal for students, professionals, and casual users. With a sleek design, decent performance, and long battery life, it's one of the best options for those on a budget.

Key Specifications

  • Processor: Intel Core i5-1235U / AMD Ryzen 5 5500U
  • Graphics: Intel Iris Xe / AMD Radeon Graphics
  • Display: 15.6" FHD (1920x1080) TN/IPS, 250 nits Brightness
  • RAM: 8GB / 16GB DDR4 (Upgradeable)
  • Storage: 256GB / 512GB / 1TB SSD
  • Battery: Up to 9 Hours
  • Weight: 3.5 lbs (1.6 kg)
  • Ports: 1x USB-C, 2x USB-A, HDMI, SD Card Reader, 3.5mm Audio Jack
  • OS: Windows 11 Home

Unique Features

  • Privacy Shutter Webcam: Built-in cover for extra security.
  • Rapid Charge Technology: Get up to 2 hours of battery life in just 15 minutes.
  • Quiet & Cooling Mode: Adjusts fan speed for silent operation.
  • Lightweight Design: Easy to carry around for students and professionals.
  • Affordable Performance: Decent performance at a budget price.

Pros & Cons

Pros:

  • Good performance for everyday tasks.
  • Lightweight and easy to carry.
  • Privacy-focused webcam with shutter.
  • Upgradable RAM and storage for better performance.

Cons:

  • Display quality is average.
  • No backlit keyboard in base models.
  • Plastic build may not feel premium.
